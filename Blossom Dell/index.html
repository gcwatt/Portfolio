THE SANCTUARY




Welcome to Blossom Dell Sanctuary, a natural preserve on 144 privet, gorgeous acres that offer sanctuary to thousands of wildlife,
forest and flower species as well as to mankind wishing to re-connect and renew the spirit within this idyllic setting.
The Sanctuary is just 80 minutes from New York City, a short drive from city life, but is entirely removed from it.
Blossom Dell Sanctuary rests on the border of Orange and Sullivan counties also known as “The Gateway to the Catskills”.
Whatever your vision for your special day, you can be assured that the overwhelming beauty
of the land will enhance and enchant this most sacred of events.

YOUR RECEPTION



The Sanctuary acts as a wildlife refuge and forest preserve to countless native animals, plants and wildflower species. Another cause fostered here at the sanctuary is the care and rescue of horses. Our horses are not only able to thrive here, but provide members and guests much joy while visiting.
Our horse run and stable create an atmosphere for unique bridal photos.
A crystal clear rambling river makes it’s way throughout the property, creating a breathtaking back drop and stunning views of The Shuwagunk Mountains.

A bon Fire area for continued evening celebrations.
